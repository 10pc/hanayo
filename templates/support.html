Ripple is not cheap to run.

While we provide this service for free, we still have to pay for it, and if we always took the money out of our own pockets, 1. we would be broke, 2. we wouldn't be able to upgrade our hardware to accomodate our users. While we do have loads of software optimisations that allow us to have Ripple run crazy fast while still being on not so high-end hardware, the more users we have, the higher-end our hardware needs to be. So we are asking you for help on this :).

We are still students.

Ripple developers, and also most of the Ripple staff, is still not working. This means that we get most of our income as students from doing small jobs as "freelancers" every so often, or, you know, allowance.

We do not have ads. And never will.

You may have adblock enabled and have not noticed, but we don't have ads anywhere on our website. We believe ads are bad, as they can be very annoying to you.

We love picking random icons from our icon library.
